About Us

Our Story

My journey in parenthood wasn't a traditional one. I was just 20, in the middle of college, when my future took a complete turn from the one that I had planned. My pregnancy was not part of my original plan in life, but she became the most important part of my life.

Even through struggles, I continued on one day at a time. I was a working mom, but also a mom who went to school at the same time. So, I would study during her naps, write papers with the baby monitor on my desk, and I will probably graduate with a degree in one hand and my little girl's hand in the other. I'm excited for life after graduation.

I currently work as a delivery driver while I continue my education.

I wanted to create this safe space because I know what it feels like to feel alone and to search for answers in the middle of the night, and to wonder if you're even doing anything right.

Why Was This Website Created?

This is for all the parents who are trying to navigate their own unexpected journeys, the ones who are juggling school and sleepless nights, the ones trying to find their new role in this world, and the ones who just need to know they aren't the only one and they are NOT alone!
