Our Story

My journey in parenthood wasn't a traditional one. I was just 20, in the middle of college, when my future took a complete turn from the one I had planned. My pregnancy was not part of my original plan in life, but she became the most important part of my life.

Even through struggles, I continued on one day at a time. I was a working mom, but also a mom who went to school at the same time. I studied during her naps, wrote papers with the baby monitor on my desk, and I will probably graduate with a degree in one hand and my little girl's hand in the other. I'm excited for life after graduation.

I currently work as a delivery driver while I continue my education.

I created this space because I know what it feels like to feel alone, to search for answers in the middle of the night, and to wonder if you're doing anything right.

What This Website Offers

On this website, you can post blogs anonymously, find parenting tips and tricks, and discover helpful resources.

Why Was This Website Created?

This is for all the parents navigating unexpected journeys, juggling school and sleepless nights, redefining their role in the world, and needing to know they are NOT alone.
